How to make pendrive bootable without any software

13 March,2015

John Steiner

10:45 PM

446 Views

For setuping windows 8 on your pc there are 2 steps.

  • First make your pendrive bootable.
  • Then setup your windows by this bootable pendrive.

For making pendrive bootable you need a minimum 4gb pendrive.Now insert your pendrive in your pc.Right click on your pendrive.Now format your pendrive.Now press windows+r button together.This action open run.Now type diskpart on run and press enter.Now type this commands and press enter.

  • list disk
  • select disk 1
  • clean
  • create partition primary
  • format recommended
  • active
  • exit

Your pendrive is now bootable.
